About Us

Forever Ballet is a dance studio for adult ballet dancers. We aim to bring ballet enthusiasts, former performers and ballet fans together to practice and learn about Ballet. There are classical and contemporary ballet classes and you will also be able to join Yoga, Pilates, Ballet based Fitness and strength training classes.
